Private by default

No account. No ads. No tracking.

BoardLink is built so you can play without handing over your identity. The initial release ships without any of the things that usually come bundled with "free."

  • ✓ No account, email address, or phone number required.
  • ✓ No advertising SDK, analytics SDK, or third-party tracking scripts.
  • ✓ No social login and no public free-text chat.
  • ✓ Raw visitor IP addresses are never persisted by the app.
  • ✓ Server-authoritative state — no client is trusted for rules or scores.
